# Portfolio Positioning

This project is a simulated industrial customer-experience and service analytics lab.

It demonstrates how I structure:

- customer-experience analytics and stakeholder reporting;
- data validation and SQL-style reporting;
- simulated industrial telemetry pipelines;
- anomaly detection and service-risk scoring;
- API-style scoring and local production simulation;
- transparent limitations and human-review boundaries.

The strongest fit is for internships or junior roles in Applied AI, Data Analytics, Industrial AI, AI/Product Operations, Data Engineering and Responsible AI documentation.

All data and operational scenarios are simulated. The project does not claim affiliation, endorsement, internal data access, production deployment, or real industrial integration with any organization.
