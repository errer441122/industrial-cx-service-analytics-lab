# Customer Satisfaction Brief

## Executive Readout

The simulated CX sample shows a weighted customer satisfaction rate of 65.9% across 1,055 feedback-volume units. Satisfaction is strongest for Urban Commuters, Touring Owners, and Performance Enthusiasts. First-Time Owners are the only at-risk segment, driven by purchase financing clarity, digital onboarding, and ownership documentation friction.

## Segment Insight

First-Time Owners need the clearest operational attention. The segment has low satisfaction in purchase and ownership moments, especially where financing, insurance, warranty documentation, and onboarding guidance are unclear. Completed follow-ups improve outcomes, but two open follow-ups keep the segment at risk.

## Recommended Actions

1. Standardize first-purchase documentation and financing comparison templates.
2. Add dealer callback triggers for first-time owners with friction score 4 or 5.
3. Track onboarding completion inside the CX dashboard as a weekly operational metric.
4. Review South-region first-time owner records in the next cross-functional CX meeting.

## Stakeholder Handoff

CX owns the insight brief, dealers own callback completion, digital owns onboarding content clarity, and CRM owns follow-up tracking. The next dashboard check should compare satisfaction rate and post-action delta for First-Time Owners after the documentation fix.
