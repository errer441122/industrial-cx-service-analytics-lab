# Limitations

- All Ducati/CX and industrial telemetry data is simulated.
- OPC UA, MQTT, InfluxDB, Grafana, MLflow, MinIO, Docker, and Slurm files are local portfolio artifacts unless a separate smoke log states they were executed.
- No real BI-REX, CINECA, IT4LIA, Ducati, plant, dealer, edge, cloud, GPU, or HPC execution is claimed.
- Service-risk outputs are advisory and require human review.
